Software Engineering Leadership

Hi

I help organizations build digital products & the teams behind said products. I enjoy socio-technological challenges, growing teams across multiple countries, crossing industries boundaries, and managing a diverse set of people, technologies and processes.

During the last decades, I've built meaningful digital products people use everyday. My passion is bridging business, people, and technology.

I'm a software engineer at heart and businessperson by training.

I'm an alum of Columbia University (USA), London Business School (UK), and Unicamp (Brazil) where I received my MBAs and MSc respectively.

Interests

In my free time I'm an avid ultra-distance runner & board game enthusiast.

You may find me hiking, apple-picking or enjoying a carefuly brewed cortado in the charming Hudson Valley area of the New York State where I've chosen to live with my wife, son, and retired greyhound.

Experience

I've worn many hats in my past life: Partner at Work & Co (USA), Software Consultant at Digitalist (Finland), Sr. Executive at Nokia (Finland & Germany), Sr. Director at Viasat (USA) and a CTO/Entrepreneur in Europe & Brazil.

While leading these organizations, I had the privilege of working with numerous Fortune 500 companies, tech startups, and non-profits on a wide range of innovative software and hardware projects.
