Tiago Luchini

Chief Technology Officer | High Growth Company Leader | Digital Business Strategic Operator
Team Builder | Thought Leader

Tiago Luchini is a CTO, Digital Product Innovation Strategist, Business Leader, and Engineer with credits including leading Virgin America's award-winning digital transformation, Apple's "Today at Apple" program, and serving as Lead Architect for YouTube Music and YouTube TV while a Founding Partner at the award-winning digital solutions company, Work & Co.

He is the recipient of 4 Cannes Lions, receiving 1st Place Titanium and Grand Prix awards – the two highest honors – for his Apple work, has won 5 Webby Awards, and 3 Pixel Awards, in addition to Clio, One Show, Communication Arts, DES Innovation and Digiday Awards.

As a Business and Operations Executive, Tiago is passionate about creating structures and processes where work gets done efficiently with first-class architecture and code. This has enabled his success assembling and managing high-performing global teams of engineers during hypergrowth for large public companies and start-ups in biotech, e-commerce, and satellite/communications.

Career Highlights & Leadership Impact include:

  • High-Performing Global Team Builder: Record of success building large teams of engineers and product managers with an emphasis on training and retention while creating measurable productivity KPIs. 20+ years of identifying talent and leading first-class engineers around the world, solving complex engineering, cloud, app, ML, AI challenges.
  • C-Level Strategist & Solutions Architect Working with C-Level Counterparts at Apple, Google, Twitter, Virgin America and More: Created award-winning programs and solutions for the worlds leading technology and consumer brands, developing strategies, roadmaps and executing work. Awards include:
    • Winner of Cannes Lion, Webby, Clio for Virgin America’s complete digital transformation.
    • Winner of Cannes Lion for Apple’s "Today at Apple" program launch in 498 stores worldwide.
  • P&L, Management and Operations Responsibility: Proven success creating the infrastructure for teams and departments to excel with an eye on profitability, accountability, and market-leading ROI. Account management, client relations, new business development, negotiating, department structuring, operations creation and continuous improvements.
  • Subject Matter Expert & Public Speaker: Invited speaker for digital and developer conferences and contributing writer for issues related to security, deep learning, blockchain, and app development.


In his free time, Tiago is an avid ultra-distance runner & board game enthusiast.

You may find him hiking, apple-picking, or enjoying a carefully brewed cortado in the charming Hudson Valley region of New York State where he lives with his wife & retired greyhound.

Tiago currently serves as Chief Technology Officer at Deep Origin, a biotech startup, since August 2022, where he built a team of world-class engineers in 6 months and then successfully produced an MVP providing cloud engineering and IT services for life sciences and biotech companies in 3 months.

Previous to this, Tiago served as CTO at Odeko, a $250M funded startup providing tools for businesses to operate and grow, from 2021 to 2022. And from 2019 to 2021 he served as Head of Digital Transformation at Viasat, a 5000+ employee public company providing internet satellite for airlines and the US military, where he led a cross-functional global team of 90+ engineers and product managers in charge of digital, architectural, and organization transformation.

From 2013 to 2019, Tiago was Partner of Technology & Digital Innovation at Work & Co. where he was responsible for defining the technical strategy, vision, processes, and team culture working for clients including Facebook, Google, Apple, Twitter, Pepsi, Disney, and Virgin America. Here he built and managed a global team of 110+ engineers, product managers and strategists from scratch.

Tiago is an industry thought leader and speaker and has written for publications including Ad Age and The Next Web on issues including blockchain, AI, and apps.

Tiago earned dual MBAs from Columbia Business School and the London Business School in addition to a Master of Science in Strategic Management Information Systems from Universidade Estadual De Campinas in Brazil.
